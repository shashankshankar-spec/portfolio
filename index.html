Project 02 — Branding & Research

Echo & Verse

Brand Rationale

Echo & Verse was an academic branding project focused on bridging music and poetry, positioning song lyrics as cultural texts comparable to traditional literary forms.

Research Insight

Research highlighted the importance of strong visual signifiers in digital content. Audiences responded more actively to bold, memorable identity systems, which informed the brand’s visual and tonal direction.

Visual Identity

The brand adopted loud, high-contrast typography and colour choices, executed with intent rather than excess, to reinforce memorability and cultural presence.
